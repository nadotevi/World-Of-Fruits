Pineapple: The Crown Jewel of Tropical Delight

In the world of tropical fruits, the pineapple stands tall and proud, sporting its spiky crown and offering a burst of sweet, tangy flavor. Today, we bring you an exclusive interview with the fruit itself, as well as a playful column of pineapple-themed riddles. Let's dive into the juicy world of pineapples!

An Interview with the Pineapple: The Fruit with a Crown

Interviewer: Greetings, Pineapple! Thank you for gracing us with your presence. How does it feel to be the crown jewel of tropical fruits?
Pineapple: Ah, it's a delight! I like to think of myself as the royalty of the fruit kingdom. The crown on my head may be a bit prickly, but it adds that regal touch.
Interviewer: Absolutely regal! Speaking of your crown, does it symbolize anything special in the pineapple world?
Pineapple: Indeed! My crown is not just for show. It represents hospitality and welcome in many cultures. In the old days, people would place a pineapple at their doorstep to signal that guests were always welcome.
